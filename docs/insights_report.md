# Insights Report

## Executive Insights

- Total applications analyzed: 720.
- The funnel starts with 720 applications and reaches 140 hires.
- 601 candidates reached Shortlisted stage.
- 444 candidates reached Interview 1.
- 192 candidates reached Offer stage.
- 140 candidates were hired.

## Funnel Insights

The largest drop-off occurs between Interview 1 and Interview 2, where the funnel narrows from 444 to 282 candidates. This may indicate a need to improve screening quality before interview scheduling or to review interview criteria.

## Source Insights

Source Quality Score is designed to avoid rewarding only high-volume sources. It uses a weighted score across shortlist rate, interview rate, and hire rate. This helps identify sources that bring better downstream quality, not just more applicants.

## Recruiter Productivity Insights

Recruiter Productivity Score combines hires, offers, interviews, shortlisted candidates, and workload. This helps TA leaders understand both volume and quality of recruiter pipelines.

## Skills Intelligence

Skill intelligence identifies which skills are most common among applicants and which skills convert better to shortlist/hire stages. This can guide sourcing strategies and market intelligence research.

## Automation Insight

The weekly reporting script turns processed CSVs into a markdown summary, which can be adapted into automated emails, Teams messages, or Power Automate workflows.
